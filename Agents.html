Realtor


Hi! I'm Vanessa and I am number the #1 residential real-estate agent of Durham Region. Born and raised in the Durham Region, I have watched the area grow into the vibrant communities that exist today. I became a real estate agent in 2014, with a serious passion for assisting others in achieving their goals.As the real estate industry evolves to become more innovative and efficient, so do I. With the ever changing market, it is essential to adapt and cater to the different needs of my clients. I believe in creating a strategic plan, advising on the best way to present their home for the market and offering access to my group of professionals – such as stagers, mortgage brokers, lawyers, home inspectors, contractors and more. I truly understand that buying or selling a home can be overwhelming, so I am by my client’s side to protect their interests and assist them through every step of the process! It really is all part of providing that seamless experience.
